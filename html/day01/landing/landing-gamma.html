Gamma sneaks up the bush, when suddenly a wild mutated rabbit jumps out of it and bites the dog. Gamma is able to kick the rabbit away and run back to you. However, the bite has infected Gamma with fast-acting poison, stopping his heart.

You search your bag for a potential cure and inject Gamma with epinephrine that jolts Gamma back to life. You quickly clean and wrap up the infected area and put Gamma on the back seat of your hoverbike.

You walk to the bush and collect fur and other samples to analyze and add to your device's data.

Creature Update: Gamma gains immunity to poison and learns the ability Poison Fang: protracts fangs and lands a poisonous bite on an enemy.
