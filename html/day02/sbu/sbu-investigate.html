You walk around campus and spot some smoke and hear some music in the distance. You follow it, finding a group of humans with tribal masks chanting, circling a dead animal over a fire.

One faces and points at you, and the others turn their heads. They start talking amongst themselves in a language similar to English, but you don't understand it.

They start walking towards you, so you try and say a greeting and wave, but they continue to creep towards you, so you slowly back away.

All of a sudden one jumps at you, so you run away.
