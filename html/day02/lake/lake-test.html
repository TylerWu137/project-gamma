You test the water using your device, and it reads that the water is highly toxic. You start packing your things and walking away, but then you spot Gamma panting, approaching the side of the lake to take a sip of the water. Before you can stop him, he takes one huge gulp.

You quickly pull him away before he can drink any more water when suddenly, his eyes begin to glow a dark green. Unsure of what kind of properties the lake water has, you carefully track Gamma's health.

Since the water didn't kill Gamma, you take some of the water with you in case of emergencies.
