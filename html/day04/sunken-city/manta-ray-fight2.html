Gamma charges at the manta ray with immense speed. Shocked that Gamma is able to see it, the manta ray reacts too late, and Gamma lands a direct hit.

Gamma quickly rushes over to you, and you hand him a mutated vine. He swims towards the manta ray and releases the vine, which emits a calming mist and tames the manta ray.

Manta Ray has been tamed! Abilities:

  • Aqua Veil: secretes a fluid that renders itself invisible underwater
  • Slipstream: surrounds itself with condensed water and charges at the enemy
