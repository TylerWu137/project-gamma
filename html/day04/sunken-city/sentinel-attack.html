Agitated, the Sentinel uses Gravitide Slam: it lifts its massive stone limbs and slams them to the ground, sending shockwaves through the seafloor.

The seafloor explodes beneath Gamma, sending him flying and hitting a wall.

You swim to Gamma and assess the damage.

The Sentinel walks towards you, and in a bellowing voice, it speaks to you:

“I am the Sentinel, protector of this sacred museum. Why have you come here to disturb my peace?”
