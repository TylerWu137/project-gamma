You continue to swim around the sunken city, and spot a ruined museum overgrown with mutated corals, seaweed, and algae.

A magnificent statue of a dinosaur guards the entrance to the museum, but parts of it have unfortunately been crumbled, eroded, or overgrown by wild oceanic plants. Intrigued, you enter.

You move forward, visiting the exhibits showcasing different periods of Earth, dating back to its creation, to the dinosaurs of the Mesozoic Era, the ancient civilizations in the Ancient Era, the Middle Ages, the Reformation and Scientific Revolution, all the way to the Modern Era of Technology!

As you circle back to the entrace of the museum, you see a flash in the statue's eyes. It suddenly comes to life and charges toward you.
